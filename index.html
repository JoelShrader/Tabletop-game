Before rushing headlong into character creation, take time to familiarize yourself with modern life and what led to it in the setting. As a homebrew of my own design it's got its own quirks, opportunities, and restrictions. You should also familiarize yourself with OVA Revized to get an idea of what you're expected to come up with. As the setting features a wide variety of options for what to play, the list of options will be non-exhaustive. Like the ruleset, it's pretty open ended. If you can justify to me why you should be able to play it, I'll most likely let you do so.

If you aren't sure if an idea works, or want help making something fit, or simply want to brainstorm, contact me via email or on Discord. Make sure you are set up with a Roll20 account at some point before game time. I will distribute a game link via Discord when it is set up.
